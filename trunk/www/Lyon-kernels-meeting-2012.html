L(yo)nBox 2012

LinBox high performance kernels meeting

July 16th - 21st, 2012, LIP, ENS-Lyon, Lyon, France
Schedule:

This workshop will be very informal. The subject is high performance kernels for exact linear algebra including the use of code generation for high performance. All those involved in HPAC and/or interested in the subject are welcome to join.

Some people may participate for the entire week, others for a day or two. More are likely to attend toward the end of the week (days just before ISSAC) than earlier. So that we may share information about our intentions, please doodle your intended dates of attendance at http://www.doodle.com/brdzt3errcicn89e. Note that no centralized decisions or date setting will be based on this doodle other than perhaps the timing of a couple of talks. If you would like to give a talk, contact saunders@udel.edu.

In particular the team of a NSF project at Delaware, Drexel, and Morehouse Universities will take this opportunity to coordinate their projects and learn about related HPAC work.
